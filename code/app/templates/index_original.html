  About Us

  Mission:   To intelligently recommend healthy, sustainable products to conscious consumers.

  consha is a free browser plugin that makes it easier for people to find healthy, sustainable alternatives of
  the products they purchase. The browser extension has two main features. First, it provides a green score for the product,
  which tells shoppers how safe the product is for them and the planet. Second, if the product is not healthy and sustainable,
  the user will see a list of similar recommended products from the same site that are from verified sustainable companies.
